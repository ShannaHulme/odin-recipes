Rancher's Salad

Return Home

Description

They might actually eat this one. Each component of this salad is a common food item, but mixed together makes for an uninspiring dish.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound elbow macaroni
  • Boiling salted water
  • 1 can (1 pound) kidney beans
  • 4 beef frankfurters
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup chili sauce
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Dash pepper
  • Few drops Tabasco sauce
  • 1 tablespoon prepared horse-radish
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Crisp salad greens

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water according to package instructions, until tender.
  2. Drain; rinse with cold water and chill in a large bowl.
  3. Drain kidney beans; discard liquid.
  4. Cut frankfurters into thin diagonal slices.
  5. Add beans and frankfurters to macaroni.
  6. Combine sour cream and next 8 ingredients.
  7. Pour sour cream mixture over the macaroni mixture; toss until thoroughly mixed.
  8. Turn salad into a bowl lined with crisp salad greens.

Makes 8 servings
